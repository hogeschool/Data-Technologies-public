# Higher Normal Forms (4NF, 5NF, 6NF)

## Fourth Normal Form (4NF)
- Eliminates **multi-valued dependencies**.

## Fifth Normal Form (5NF)
- Ensures **join dependencies** are properly structured.

## Sixth Normal Form (6NF)
- Further minimizes redundancy in advanced systems.
