# Anomalies in Unnormalized Databases

## Types of Anomalies
1. **Update Anomaly** – Inconsistent data updates.
2. **Insert Anomaly** – Difficulty adding new data without redundant entries.
3. **Delete Anomaly** – Loss of vital data when removing records.

## Example
Without normalization, updating a customer’s phone number across multiple entries leads to inconsistencies.
